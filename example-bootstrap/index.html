What I Offer

Responsive

In today's mobile-driven world, having a responsive website is crucial. I can optimize your website to adapt seamlessly across various devices, ensuring that your content is accessible and engaging for users on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

UX/UI design

I specialize in creating intuitive and visually appealing user interfaces (UI) and optimizing user experiences (UX). By understanding user behavior and employing industry best practices, I ensure that your website or application is both aesthetically pleasing and easy to navigate.

Graphic design

With a strong background in graphic design, I can help you create eye-catching visuals that elevate your brand. From logo design and branding materials to digital illustrations and infographics, I ensure that your visual assets reflect your unique identity and captivate your audience.

Site audits

If you already have a website but want to improve its performance, I offer comprehensive website audits. I analyze factors such as site speed, accessibility, user experience, and search engine optimization, providing detailed insights and actionable recommendations for enhancing your website.

Wireframing

Before diving into development, I can create wireframes and interactive prototypes to visualize the user flow and functionality of your website or application. This iterative process helps to refine the design and ensure that the final product meets your expectations.

Web development

From front-end to back-end, I offer comprehensive web development services. Whether you need a static website, an e-commerce platform, or a custom web application, I can bring your vision to life with clean, efficient, and responsive code.

Site maintenance

Your online presence requires ongoing maintenance and updates. I offer reliable website maintenance services, including routine backups, security enhancements, content updates, and bug fixes, so that your website remains secure, up-to-date, and functional at all times.
